## Traversal Seed Guidance

The traversal-cap recommendation is size-dependent. Small-`N` prepare-state
profiling favored a leaner seed, while larger `N` favored the larger-cap stable
seed among the tested configs.

- `N <= 65536`: `65536/64/4096/1024`. Prepare-state profiling on March 20, 2026
  found the lean traversal seed faster and stable at `65,536` particles on GPU 3.
- `N > 65536`: `262144/256/8192/4096`. The larger-cap seed remained the best
  stable choice among tested configs at `131,072` and `262,144` particles.

For the underlying crossover measurements, see
[`docs/runtime_traversal_comparison_2026-03-20.md`](/export/home/tbuck/jaccpot/docs/runtime_traversal_comparison_2026-03-20.md).
